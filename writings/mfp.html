Mentor for Philly is an education non-profit organization that focuses on training high school students with the "social-know-how" skills for the college application process, and their eventual college experience. Oscar Wang, CEO of MFP, has been a good friend since my days at Haverford College, so I was happy to volunteer as a grant writer and strategic consultant for MFP since January 2015. I am now excited to offer my new skills as a developer to build the MFP website, which will allow students to enjoy features, such as:

  • Search for college mentors based on specialty areas.
  • Compare net cost of potential college tuitions.
  • Access top college search resources.

More features and updates soon to come!
